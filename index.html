Weaving the Memory of the Sea and Town

DAISEN UMIHOTARU stands at the crossroads of nature and culture, where the rhythmic waves of the Sea of Japan meet the quiet dignity of Daisen Town. Here, in the coastal hamlet of Mikuriya, we've created more than a villa‚Äîwe've crafted a portal to authentic Japanese coastal living.

Our name, "Umihotaru" (Êµ∑Ëõç), meaning "sea firefly," captures the ephemeral beauty of bioluminescent moments along these shores. Like the gentle glow of fireflies dancing above summer waves, we illuminate the path to genuine connection with this storied landscape.

Just steps from your door, local fishermen mend their nets at dawn. The JR Nawa Station connects you to the region's treasures, while the fishing port offers glimpses into centuries-old maritime traditions. This is where your story becomes woven into the fabric of coastal Japan.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the house rules?

We ask all guests to respect our home and neighbors: No parties or events. Quiet hours 10:00 PM - 7:00 AM. No shoes indoors (slippers provided). Please dispose of trash according to local recycling guidelines (instructions provided). Treat the space as your own home with care and respect.

Is smoking allowed?

The villa is strictly non-smoking indoors. Smoking is permitted on the outdoor terrace only. Please use the provided ashtray and dispose of cigarette butts properly.

Can I bring pets?

We love pets! Small to medium-sized dogs and cats are welcome with advance approval (¬•5,000 pet fee per stay). Please inform us during booking. We ask that pets be house-trained, not left alone in the villa, and kept off furniture and beds. Pet sheets and waste disposal bags are the guest's responsibility.

What about noise and neighbors?

DAISEN UMIHOTARU is located in a peaceful residential area near the fishing port. We ask guests to be considerate of neighbors, especially during quiet hours (10:00 PM - 7:00 AM). The terrace can be enjoyed, but please keep music and voices at a respectful volume.

Is there a minimum stay requirement?

Minimum stay is 1 night year-round. During peak seasons (Golden Week, Obon, New Year), a 2-night minimum may apply. Extended stays of 7+ nights qualify for special rates‚Äîplease contact us directly.

What if I need to cancel my reservation?

See our Cancellation Policy below for full details. In summary: 30+ days before: full refund minus processing fee. 14-29 days: 50% refund. 7-13 days: 25% refund. Less than 7 days: no refund. We recommend travel insurance for maximum flexibility.

Do you provide meals?

We do not provide in-house dining, but offer breakfast delivery service from a local caf√© and can arrange BBQ equipment rental. The villa has a fully equipped kitchen for self-catering. We're happy to recommend nearby restaurants and make reservations on your behalf.

Is there a convenience store or supermarket nearby?

Yes! A convenience store is 5 minutes by car, and a full supermarket is 10 minutes away. We also offer pre-arrival grocery shopping service (¬•2,000 + cost of groceries). The local fishing port offers fresh seafood (seasonal availability).

Can I check in early or check out late?

Subject to availability. Early check-in (from 1:00 PM): ¬•5,000. Late check-out (until 1:00 PM): ¬•5,000. Please request at least 48 hours in advance. We'll do our best to accommodate your schedule.

What if I have an emergency during my stay?

We provide 24/7 emergency support via phone and messaging app (LINE/WhatsApp). Our local team can respond quickly. Emergency contact information is provided in your welcome guide. Nearest hospital: Saihaku Hospital (15 min by car).
